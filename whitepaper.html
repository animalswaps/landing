ZooToken : A Deflating Token for Deflating Tokens

Abstract

(Draft)
Problems: 
- lack of liquidity even if automatically adding to AMM for deflating coins
- prices of them are very unstable
- high price slippage
- holders are similar group of users


Solution:
- adding a bridging tool as a token
- with same features like safemoon or pig token
- even higher liquidity pooling on transfer to punish more of weak hands 
- adding safemoon, safemars, ... top 5 deflating coins / ZOO for cheaper transactions

Vision:
To be a utility tool for all Deflating-coin lovers.
